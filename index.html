      MODEL PERFORMANCE

      Held-out test accuracy.

      Time-based 75 / 10 / 15 split (train / calibration / test). Test = the most recent slice of matches the model has never seen during training. No random shuffling — past predicts future.

      Calibration target: when the model says x%, it should be right ~x% of the time. ECE = mean |predicted − observed| over 10 probability bins.

      METHODOLOGY

      How predictions are built.

      1. Ingest. CricSheet zips for T20, IT20, ODI, Test and major franchise leagues — over 13,500 matches and 5.5 million balls — loaded into a local DuckDB.
      2. Extract announced XI. Re-walk every cached JSON to pull info.players[team] = […] into a match_xi table. This is the highest-leverage signal: knowing the lineup pre-toss.
      3. Engineer leakage-safe features. Per-format Elo (margin-weighted K), rolling form (last 3/5/10/20), time-aware H2H, AS-OF venue stats, top-7 batter / top-5 bowler career aggregates from the announced XI.
      4. Stacked ensemble. LightGBM-numeric, LightGBM-with-cats, XGBoost, CatBoost, Logistic Regression. Outputs blended via a logistic-regression meta-learner trained on the calibration slice.
      5. Verify. Time-based test set; re-split sanity checks (2023+, 2024+, 2025+ cutoffs) confirm 70-76% accuracy band on T20 with ECE ≤ 3.5%.
